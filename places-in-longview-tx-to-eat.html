Places To Eat In Longview, Tx

Introduction

If you're looking for great food in Longview, Texas, you're in luck! There are plenty of amazing restaurants to choose from, no matter what you're in the mood for. From classic Southern cuisine to international flavors, Longview has it all. In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the best places to eat in the city.

1. Bodacious Bar-B-Q

If you're in the mood for some delicious, authentic Texas barbecue, you can't go wrong with Bodacious Bar-B-Q. This local favorite has been serving up mouth-watering brisket, ribs, and more for over 50 years. Don't forget to try their famous homemade sausage!

2. Luigi's Italian Cafe

For a taste of Italy in the heart of Longview, head to Luigi's Italian Cafe. This cozy restaurant serves up classic dishes like spaghetti carbonara, lasagna, and chicken fettuccine alfredo. Don't forget to save room for dessert - their tiramisu is out of this world.

3. The Catch

If you're in the mood for some fresh seafood, you can't go wrong with The Catch. This casual restaurant offers a variety of dishes, from shrimp and oysters to catfish and crawfish. Make sure to try their famous seafood gumbo - it's a local favorite.

4. Johnny Cace's Seafood & Steakhouse

For a more upscale dining experience, check out Johnny Cace's Seafood & Steakhouse. This elegant restaurant has been serving up delicious steaks and seafood since 1949. Don't miss their famous cheese soup - it's a must-try.

5. La Michoacana Meat Market

If you're in the mood for some authentic Mexican food, head to La Michoacana Meat Market. This grocery store and restaurant offers a variety of dishes, from tacos and burritos to tamales and menudo. Don't forget to grab some fresh salsa and chips to go!

6. Jucy's Hamburgers

Sometimes, you just need a good old-fashioned burger - and Jucy's Hamburgers is the place to go. This local chain has been serving up juicy burgers and crispy fries since 1980. Make sure to try their signature Jucy's Original Burger - it's a classic for a reason.

7. Willie's Place

For some classic Southern comfort food, head to Willie's Place. This cozy restaurant serves up dishes like chicken fried steak, meatloaf, and fried catfish. Don't forget to try their homemade pies - they're the perfect way to end a meal.

8. Pizza King

If you're in the mood for some pizza, head to Pizza King. This local chain has been serving up delicious pies since 1965. Don't miss their signature King's Special Pizza - it's topped with pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, and green peppers.

9. The Butcher Shop

For some of the best steak in town, head to The Butcher Shop. This upscale restaurant offers a variety of cuts, from filet mignon to porterhouse. Don't forget to try their famous onion rings - they're the perfect side dish.

10. The Coffee Mill

Looking for a cozy spot for breakfast or brunch? Head to The Coffee Mill. This local favorite serves up delicious omelets, pancakes, and breakfast sandwiches. Don't forget to try their homemade cinnamon rolls - they're a must-try.

Conclusion

No matter what you're in the mood for, Longview, TX has plenty of amazing restaurants to choose from. From classic Southern cuisine to international flavors, you're sure to find something that satisfies your cravings. So why not plan a foodie tour of the city and try them all?
