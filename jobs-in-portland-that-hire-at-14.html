Jobs In Portland That Hire At 14

Introduction

Are you a 14-year-old living in Portland and looking for a job? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will go over some of the best jobs in Portland that hire at 14. Whether you're looking for a summer gig or a part-time job during the school year, we've got you covered.

1. Babysitting

One of the most popular jobs for 14-year-olds is babysitting. Many parents in Portland are looking for responsible teenagers who can watch their kids while they're away. Babysitting is a great way to earn some extra cash and gain valuable experience in childcare.

2. Dog Walking

If you love dogs, then dog walking may be the perfect job for you. Many dog owners in Portland are busy during the day and need someone to walk their furry friends. As a dog walker, you'll get to spend time outdoors and get some exercise while earning money.

3. Yard Work

Another great job for 14-year-olds is yard work. Many homeowners in Portland need help with tasks such as mowing the lawn, weeding, and watering plants. If you're willing to put in some hard work, you can earn a decent amount of money doing yard work.

4. Retail

If you're interested in working in a store, there are many retail jobs in Portland that hire at 14. You can work as a sales associate, cashier, or stocker. Retail jobs can be a great way to gain customer service experience and learn valuable skills such as cash handling.

5. Food Service

Many restaurants and cafes in Portland also hire 14-year-olds to work as bussers, dishwashers, or food runners. These jobs can be fast-paced and require you to be on your feet for long periods of time, but they can also be very rewarding.

6. Tutoring

If you excel in a particular subject, you can offer your tutoring services to other students in Portland. Many parents are willing to pay for extra help for their kids, especially in subjects like math and science. Tutoring can be a great way to earn money while helping others.

7. Lifeguarding

If you're a strong swimmer and have your lifeguard certification, you can work as a lifeguard at one of Portland's many pools or beaches. Lifeguarding can be a fun and rewarding job, but it also requires a lot of responsibility and attention to detail.

8. Camp Counselor

During the summer months, many camps in Portland hire 14-year-olds to work as counselors. As a camp counselor, you'll be responsible for leading activities, supervising campers, and ensuring their safety. This job can be a lot of fun and a great way to make new friends.

9. Pet Sitting

If you're an animal lover, you can also offer your pet sitting services to people in Portland. Many pet owners are looking for someone to watch their pets while they're away, and they're willing to pay for it. Pet sitting can be a great way to earn money while spending time with furry friends.

10. Photography

Finally, if you have a talent for photography, you can offer your services as a photographer in Portland. Many people need professional photos taken for events, such as weddings and graduations. Photography can be a fun and creative job that allows you to showcase your skills.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are many jobs in Portland that hire at 14. Whether you're interested in babysitting, dog walking, yard work, retail, food service, tutoring, lifeguarding, camp counseling, pet sitting, or photography, there's something for everyone. So, start exploring your options and find the perfect job for you!
