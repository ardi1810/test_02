Introduction

Are you a student or someone who needs a part-time job in Sri Lanka? KFC Sri Lanka offers part-time job opportunities for those who want to earn extra income.

What is KFC Sri Lanka?

KFC Sri Lanka is a fast-food chain that offers a wide range of chicken-based products. It is one of the most popular fast-food chains in Sri Lanka, and it has many outlets across the country.

Why Work at KFC Sri Lanka?

Working at KFC Sri Lanka can be a great opportunity for those who want to develop their skills and gain work experience. It is a great place to work because of the following reasons:

Flexible Working Hours

KFC Sri Lanka offers flexible working hours, which means you can work during your free time or on weekends.

Training and Development

KFC Sri Lanka provides training and development opportunities for its employees. You can learn new skills and develop your career while working at KFC Sri Lanka.

Teamwork and Collaboration

KFC Sri Lanka promotes teamwork and collaboration among its employees. You will have the opportunity to work with a team and build strong relationships with your colleagues.

Types of Part-Time Jobs at KFC Sri Lanka

KFC Sri Lanka offers the following part-time job opportunities:

Customer Service Representative

As a customer service representative, you will be responsible for taking orders, serving customers, and handling customer complaints.

Kitchen Staff

As a kitchen staff, you will be responsible for preparing food, cooking, and maintaining the cleanliness of the kitchen.

Cashier

As a cashier, you will be responsible for handling cash transactions, providing change, and keeping the cash register balanced.

Requirements for Part-Time Jobs at KFC Sri Lanka

To apply for a part-time job at KFC Sri Lanka, you must meet the following requirements:

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old to apply for a part-time job at KFC Sri Lanka.

Education Requirement

You must have at least completed your O/Ls to apply for a part-time job at KFC Sri Lanka.

Language Requirement

You must be able to communicate effectively in English or Sinhala.

How to Apply for a Part-Time Job at KFC Sri Lanka

To apply for a part-time job at KFC Sri Lanka, you can visit the nearest KFC outlet and submit your resume or apply online through their website.

Conclusion

In conclusion, KFC Sri Lanka offers part-time job opportunities for those who want to earn extra income. It is a great place to work because of its flexible working hours, training and development opportunities, and teamwork and collaboration. If you meet the requirements, you can apply for a part-time job at KFC Sri Lanka and build your career while earning extra income.
