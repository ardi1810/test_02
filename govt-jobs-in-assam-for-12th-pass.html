Introduction

Are you a 12th pass student from Assam looking for a government job? Well, you are in luck! The Assam government offers a variety of job opportunities for 12th pass candidates in various departments. In this article, we will discuss the available job opportunities and how you can apply for them.

Job Opportunities

There are several government job opportunities for 12th pass students in Assam. Some of the popular job opportunities include:
  • Assam Police Constable
  • Assam Forest Guard
  • Assam Secretariat Junior Administrative Assistant
  • Assam Agricultural University Junior Administrative Assistant
  • Assam PWD Junior Assistant

Assam Police Constable

The Assam Police Department recruits 12th pass candidates for the post of constable. The selection process involves a physical test, written test, and an interview. The pay scale is around Rs. 14,000-49,000 with additional benefits.

Assam Forest Guard

The Assam Forest Department recruits 12th pass candidates for the post of forest guard. The selection process involves a physical test, written test, and an interview. The pay scale is around Rs. 14,000-49,000 with additional benefits.

Assam Secretariat Junior Administrative Assistant

The Assam Secretariat recruits 12th pass candidates for the post of junior administrative assistant. The selection process involves a written test and an interview. The pay scale is around Rs. 14,000-49,000 with additional benefits.

Assam Agricultural University Junior Administrative Assistant

The Assam Agricultural University recruits 12th pass candidates for the post of junior administrative assistant. The selection process involves a written test and an interview. The pay scale is around Rs. 14,000-49,000 with additional benefits.

Assam PWD Junior Assistant

The Assam PWD recruits 12th pass candidates for the post of junior assistant. The selection process involves a written test and an interview. The pay scale is around Rs. 14,000-49,000 with additional benefits.

How to Apply

To apply for these job opportunities, you need to visit the official website of the respective department and fill out the application form. You need to attach your educational certificates and other required documents. After submitting the application form, you need to pay the application fee online.

Conclusion

In conclusion, if you are a 12th pass student from Assam, you have a lot of government job opportunities available. You just need to keep an eye on the official websites of the respective departments and apply when the recruitment process starts. We hope this article helped you in understanding the available job opportunities and the application process. Good luck with your job search!
