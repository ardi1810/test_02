Introduction

If you are a student or a professional looking for a part-time job in Chennai, then Zoho Corporation can be your go-to place. Zoho is a software development company headquartered in Chennai, India, and provides a range of software solutions to businesses worldwide. The company has a reputation for providing excellent job opportunities to students and professionals alike. In this article, we will explore the part-time job opportunities available at Zoho Chennai in 2023.

Part-Time Job Opportunities at Zoho Chennai

1. Marketing Intern

Zoho Chennai offers part-time marketing internships to students pursuing a degree in marketing or related fields. As a marketing intern, you will be responsible for assisting the marketing team in various tasks such as data analysis, content creation, and social media management. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in marketing and learn about the software industry.

2. Content Writer

If you have a flair for writing, then you can apply for a part-time content writer position at Zoho Chennai. As a content writer, you will be responsible for creating engaging and informative content for the company's website, blogs, and social media platforms. This position is ideal for students pursuing a degree in journalism or English literature.

3. Customer Support Executive

Zoho Chennai is also looking for part-time customer support executives to join their team. As a customer support executive, you will be responsible for handling customer queries and providing solutions to their problems. This position is ideal for students pursuing a degree in business administration or customer service.

Benefits of Working at Zoho Chennai

Working at Zoho Chennai comes with several benefits, such as:

1. Flexible Working Hours

Zoho Chennai offers flexible working hours to its part-time employees, allowing them to balance their work and studies effectively.

2. Competitive Pay

Zoho Chennai offers competitive pay to its part-time employees, ensuring that they are compensated fairly for their work.

3. Learning Opportunities

Zoho Chennai provides ample learning opportunities to its employees, allowing them to enhance their skills and gain knowledge about the software industry.

How to Apply?

If you are interested in applying for a part-time job at Zoho Chennai, you can visit their official website and apply online. Alternatively, you can also reach out to their HR department via email or phone.

Conclusion

Zoho Chennai provides excellent part-time job opportunities to students and professionals in 2023. With a range of positions available, including marketing intern, content writer, and customer support executive, there is something for everyone. If you are looking for a flexible and rewarding part-time job, then Zoho Chennai is the place to be.
