Jobs Hiring In Valley Plaza Mall Bakersfield

Introduction

If you're looking for job opportunities in Bakersfield, California, then you should definitely check out the Valley Plaza Mall. The mall is home to over 140 stores, restaurants, and other businesses, which means there are plenty of job openings available. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the jobs hiring in Valley Plaza Mall Bakersfield in 2023.

Retail Jobs

One of the main types of jobs available in Valley Plaza Mall is retail. Stores like Macy's, H&M, and Forever 21 are always looking for sales associates, cashiers, and other retail positions. These jobs are perfect for people who love fashion and enjoy interacting with customers. Some of these positions may require previous retail experience, but many are entry-level positions that offer on-the-job training.

Food Service Jobs

Another type of job available in Valley Plaza Mall is food service. With over 20 restaurants and food establishments in the mall, there are plenty of opportunities for people with experience in the food industry. Jobs like line cooks, servers, and dishwashers are always in demand. Many of these positions offer flexible schedules, making them a great choice for students or people with other part-time jobs.

Cleaning and Maintenance Jobs

Valley Plaza Mall is a large complex, which means there are plenty of cleaning and maintenance jobs available. These jobs include custodial work, landscaping, and general maintenance tasks. While these jobs may not be glamorous, they offer steady work and the opportunity to work behind the scenes to keep the mall running smoothly.

Security Jobs

As a busy shopping center, Valley Plaza Mall also needs plenty of security personnel to keep shoppers safe. Security jobs at the mall include positions like security guards, loss prevention officers, and surveillance specialists. These jobs require a strong sense of responsibility and the ability to handle high-pressure situations.

Management Jobs

For those with more experience and education, there are also management positions available at Valley Plaza Mall. These positions include store managers, assistant managers, and other leadership roles. These jobs require previous experience in the retail or food service industries, as well as excellent organizational and leadership skills.

Conclusion

If you're looking for a job in Bakersfield, then Valley Plaza Mall is definitely worth checking out. With a wide variety of job opportunities available, there's sure to be something that fits your skills and interests. Whether you're interested in retail, food service, cleaning and maintenance, security, or management, Valley Plaza Mall has plenty of jobs hiring in 2023. So why not apply today and take the first step towards your new career?
