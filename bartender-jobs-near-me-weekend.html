Bartender Jobs Near Me Weekend: What You Need To Know In 2023

Introduction

Are you looking for weekend bartender jobs near you? Whether you're a college student looking for extra cash or a seasoned bartender seeking additional shifts, it can be challenging to find the right job opportunities. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks to help you land the perfect weekend bartending gig in 2023.

Tip #1: Network with Local Bars and Restaurants

One of the best ways to find weekend bartender jobs near you is to network with local bars and restaurants. Stop by your favorite hangouts and introduce yourself to the staff and managers. Let them know you're interested in picking up some weekend shifts and ask if they have any openings available.

Tip #1.1: Be Prepared with a Resume and References

When you're networking with local bars and restaurants, it's important to be prepared with a resume and references. Even if they aren't hiring immediately, you want to make a good impression and leave them with something to remember you by.

Tip #2: Check Online Job Boards

Another great way to find weekend bartender jobs near you is to check online job boards. Websites like Indeed, Glassdoor, and Craigslist often have listings for part-time bartender positions. Make sure to tailor your resume and cover letter to each specific job posting to increase your chances of getting hired.

Tip #2.1: Be Careful of Scams

While online job boards can be a great resource, it's important to be careful of scams. Never give out personal information or pay any fees to apply for a job. If something seems too good to be true, it probably is.

Tip #3: Attend Bartending Events and Competitions

Attending bartending events and competitions can be a fun way to network with other bartenders and potentially land a weekend job. These events often have job boards or recruiters on site, so come prepared with your resume and a positive attitude.

Tip #3.1: Brush Up on Your Bartending Skills

If you're planning on attending bartending events and competitions, make sure to brush up on your bartending skills. Practice making popular drinks and be prepared to talk about your experience and passion for the industry.

Tip #4: Be Flexible and Willing to Learn

When it comes to landing a weekend bartender job, it's important to be flexible and willing to learn. Many bars and restaurants are looking for bartenders who can work multiple shifts and adapt to different environments. Show that you're eager to learn and willing to take on new challenges.

Tip #4.1: Take Advantage of Training Opportunities

If you do land a weekend bartender job, take advantage of any training opportunities that are offered. Many bars and restaurants have their own unique drink menus and protocols, so it's important to be open to learning new things.

Conclusion

Finding weekend bartender jobs near you can be a challenge, but with the right attitude and approach, it's definitely possible. Use these tips and tricks to help you land your dream job in 2023. Remember to network, check online job boards, attend bartending events, be flexible, and always be willing to learn. Good luck!
