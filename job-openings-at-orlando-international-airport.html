Orlando International Airport Overview

Orlando International Airport is one of the busiest airports in the United States, serving millions of passengers each year. The airport is constantly growing and expanding, which means there are always new job opportunities available.

Types of Jobs Available

There are a variety of jobs available at Orlando International Airport, ranging from entry-level positions to management roles. Some of the most common jobs include customer service representatives, baggage handlers, security officers, and maintenance technicians.

Benefits of Working at Orlando International Airport

Working at Orlando International Airport comes with a variety of benefits, including competitive pay, health insurance, and retirement plans. Additionally, employees have the opportunity to work in a dynamic and exciting environment, interacting with people from all over the world.

Current Job Openings

Customer Service Representative

Orlando International Airport is currently seeking customer service representatives to assist passengers with their travel needs. Applicants should have excellent communication skills and be able to work in a fast-paced environment.

Baggage Handler

The airport is also hiring baggage handlers to ensure that passengers' luggage is properly loaded and unloaded from planes. Applicants should be able to lift heavy objects and work in all weather conditions.

Security Officer

Orlando International Airport is seeking security officers to ensure the safety of passengers and employees. Applicants should have previous experience in law enforcement or security.

Maintenance Technician

The airport is also hiring maintenance technicians to ensure that the airport facilities are properly maintained. Applicants should have experience in HVAC, plumbing, or electrical work.

How to Apply

To apply for any of the positions listed above, applicants should visit the Orlando International Airport website and search for job openings. From there, they can apply online and submit their resume and cover letter.

Conclusion

Working at Orlando International Airport can be an exciting and rewarding experience. With a variety of job openings available, there is something for everyone. If you're interested in working at the airport, be sure to check out the current job openings and apply today!
