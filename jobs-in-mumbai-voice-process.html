Exploring The World Of Voice Process Jobs In Mumbai

Best gandeng Tips and References website . Search anything about gandeng Ideas in this website.

Introduction

Mumbai, the city of dreams, is a hub for various industries, including the BPO or Business Process Outsourcing industry. The voice process sector, in particular, is an exciting field that is gaining popularity among job seekers. In this article, we will explore the world of voice process jobs in Mumbai.

What are Voice Process Jobs?

Voice process jobs involve interacting with customers over the phone to provide them with information, assistance, or to solve their queries. These jobs are prevalent in the BPO industry, which outsources customer service functions to third-party service providers.

Skills Required for Voice Process Jobs

To excel in voice process jobs, one needs to possess excellent communication skills, both verbal and written. Additionally, one must be patient, empathetic, and a good listener. Basic computer skills are also necessary to navigate through customer databases and other software.

Types of Voice Process Jobs

There are various types of voice process jobs, including customer service, technical support, sales, and telemarketing. Customer service jobs involve handling customer queries and complaints, whereas technical support jobs require assistance with technical issues. Sales and telemarketing jobs involve selling products or services over the phone.

Salary and Growth Opportunities

Voice process jobs in Mumbai offer attractive remuneration packages, including incentives and bonuses. The starting salary for a fresher in the field is around INR 15,000-20,000 per month, which can go up to INR 50,000-60,000 per month for experienced professionals. Growth opportunities are also vast, with the possibility of moving up the ladder to managerial positions.

How to Get Hired in Voice Process Jobs?

To get hired in voice process jobs, one needs to have the required skills and qualifications, including a minimum education qualification of 12th pass. Most companies also provide on-the-job training to freshers. One can apply for voice process jobs through job portals, company websites, or recruitment agencies.

Top Companies Offering Voice Process Jobs in Mumbai

There are several top companies offering voice process jobs in Mumbai, including Accenture, TCS, Infosys, HGS, Wipro, and Convergys, among others.

Current Trends in Voice Process Jobs

With the increasing use of technology, voice process jobs are evolving, and there is a growing demand for professionals with knowledge of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Additionally, remote work or work-from-home options are becoming popular, providing flexibility to employees.

Conclusion

Voice process jobs in Mumbai offer a promising career option for job seekers with the required skills and qualifications. With attractive remuneration packages and growth opportunities, it is a field worth exploring.
