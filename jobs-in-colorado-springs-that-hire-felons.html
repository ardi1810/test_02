Introduction

Getting a job is a challenging task for anyone, but it's even harder for people with criminal records. Fortunately, Colorado Springs is a city that offers a range of opportunities for felons who are looking to start over. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the types of jobs that felons can get in Colorado Springs and what steps they can take to increase their chances of success.

Companies that Hire Felons in Colorado Springs

Several companies in Colorado Springs have policies in place that allow them to hire felons. These companies include Walmart, McDonald's, and Amazon. However, it's important to note that each company has its own hiring policies, and some may be more lenient than others. Some companies may only hire felons for certain types of jobs, while others may have restrictions on the type of felony conviction that a person has.

Types of Jobs Available for Felons

While there are restrictions on the type of jobs that felons can get, there are still several options available. Some of the most common jobs that felons can get include construction work, landscaping, janitorial work, and factory jobs. These jobs typically don't require a college degree, and many are in high demand, making them an excellent option for felons who are looking to start over.

Tips for Felons Looking for a Job

If you're a felon looking for a job in Colorado Springs, there are several things that you can do to increase your chances of success. First, be honest about your criminal record. Employers are more likely to hire someone who is upfront about their past than someone who tries to hide it. Second, take advantage of job training programs. There are several programs available that can help you develop the skills you need to succeed in the workforce. Finally, be persistent. Finding a job can be a long and difficult process, but if you keep at it, you'll eventually find something that works for you.

Conclusion

While getting a job as a felon can be challenging, it's not impossible. By taking advantage of the resources available in Colorado Springs and being persistent in your job search, you can find a job that will allow you to start over and build a new life for yourself. Whether you're looking for a job in construction, landscaping, or another field, there are opportunities out there for you.
