Introduction

Working remotely has become increasingly popular over the years, especially in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. Many companies have shifted to remote work, creating more opportunities for individuals looking for flexible and remote job options. The good news is that you don't need experience to land a remote job. In this article, we'll explore some companies that are currently hiring for remote positions and don't require prior experience.

1. Amazon

Amazon is one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world and offers various remote job opportunities. They regularly hire remote customer service associates who don't require prior experience. The job typically involves answering customer inquiries via phone, chat, or email. Amazon also hires remote workers for data entry, research, and content moderation positions.

2. Lionbridge

Lionbridge is a company that provides translation and localization services to businesses worldwide. They offer remote jobs in various roles such as social media evaluators, online map quality analysts, and internet assessors. These positions don't require prior experience, and the company provides training to get you started.

3. Appen

Appen is a global company that specializes in data annotation, machine learning, and AI training data. They regularly hire remote workers for various positions, including social media evaluators, search engine evaluators, and language translators. No prior experience is required, and the company provides training to help you get started.

4. Rev

Rev is a company that offers transcription, captioning, and translation services. They hire remote workers for various roles, including transcriptionists, captioners, and translators. No prior experience is required, and the company provides training to get you started.

5. Sykes

Sykes is a company that provides customer service outsourcing solutions to businesses worldwide. They offer remote customer service representative positions that don't require prior experience. The job involves answering customer inquiries and providing support via phone, email, or chat.

6. Kelly Services

Kelly Services is a staffing company that offers various remote job opportunities. They regularly hire remote workers for administrative, data entry, customer service, and sales positions. No prior experience is required, and the company provides training to get you started.

7. TTEC

TTEC is a company that provides customer support services to businesses worldwide. They offer remote customer service representative positions that don't require prior experience. The job involves answering customer inquiries via phone, email, or chat.

8. Concentrix

Concentrix is a company that provides customer experience solutions to businesses worldwide. They regularly hire remote workers for various positions, including customer service representatives, technical support, and sales. No prior experience is required, and the company provides training to get you started.

9. TranscribeMe

TranscribeMe is a company that offers transcription services to businesses worldwide. They hire remote transcribers who don't require prior experience. The job involves transcribing audio files into text format. The company provides training to help you get started.

10. ModSquad

ModSquad is a company that provides content moderation, customer support, and social media management services to businesses worldwide. They regularly hire remote workers for various positions, including content moderators, community managers, and customer support representatives. No prior experience is required, and the company provides training to get you started.

Conclusion

Remote work is becoming increasingly popular, and many companies are hiring for remote positions. The good news is that you don't need experience to land a remote job. The companies mentioned in this article offer various remote job opportunities that don't require prior experience. With the right skills, attitude, and determination, you can find a remote job that suits your needs and goals.
