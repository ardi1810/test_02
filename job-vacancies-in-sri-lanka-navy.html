Introduction

Sri Lanka Navy is one of the branches of the Sri Lanka Armed Forces, responsible for safeguarding the nation's maritime interests. The Sri Lanka Navy offers exciting and challenging career opportunities to young men and women, who are keen to serve the country and pursue their career in the naval sector. In this article, we will discuss the job vacancies available in Sri Lanka Navy in the year 2023.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the job vacancies in Sri Lanka Navy, candidates must meet certain criteria. Candidates must be Sri Lankan citizens, and should be between 18-22 years of age. Candidates must have passed the GCE Ordinary Level (O/L) examination with six subjects, including Mathematics and English, and should have passed the GCE Advanced Level (A/L) examination with three subjects, including Mathematics.

Job Vacancies

The Sri Lanka Navy offers a range of job vacancies to suit different skill sets and interests. Some of the job vacancies available in Sri Lanka Navy in 2023 include:

Naval Officer

Naval Officers are responsible for the management and administration of Sri Lanka Navy. They are required to have strong leadership and management skills, and should be able to work under pressure. Candidates who have completed the Officer Cadet Training Course (OCTC) at the Naval and Maritime Academy (NMA) are eligible to apply for this position.

Technical Officer

Technical Officers are responsible for the maintenance and repair of naval equipment and machinery. They should have a strong technical background and should be able to work independently. Candidates who have completed a degree or diploma in engineering are eligible to apply for this position.

Seaman

Seamen are responsible for the operation of naval vessels and equipment. They should have good communication skills and should be able to work in a team. Candidates who have completed the Basic Seamanship Course (BSC) at the NMA are eligible to apply for this position.

Physical Fitness

Physical fitness is an important aspect for all job vacancies in Sri Lanka Navy. Candidates must pass the medical and physical fitness tests, which include running, swimming, and other physical activities.

Conclusion

In conclusion, job vacancies in Sri Lanka Navy offer exciting and challenging career opportunities for young men and women who are keen to serve the country and pursue their career in the naval sector. Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria and have a strong interest in naval operations can apply for various job vacancies available in Sri Lanka Navy in 2023.
