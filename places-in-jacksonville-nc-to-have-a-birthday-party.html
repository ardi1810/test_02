Top Places To Have A Birthday Party In Jacksonville, Nc

Introduction

Celebrating a birthday is an exciting event, but planning a party can be stressful. Finding the perfect place to host a birthday party can be a challenge, especially in a city like Jacksonville, NC. However, there are plenty of great options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore some of the best places in Jacksonville to throw a birthday bash.

1. Bowlarena Lanes

Bowlarena Lanes is a great place to host a birthday party for all ages. With 24 lanes, this bowling alley can accommodate large groups. They offer party packages that include bowling, shoe rental, pizza, and soft drinks. You can also add extras like arcade games, laser tag, and bumper cars to your party package.

2. Jumping Jungle

Jumping Jungle is an indoor playground that offers a variety of play areas for kids. They have bounce houses, slides, obstacle courses, and more. They offer party packages that include playtime, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like face painting, balloon animals, and a private party room.

3. Jacksonville Commons Recreation Center

The Jacksonville Commons Recreation Center is a great place to host a birthday party for active children. They offer a variety of activities, including basketball, volleyball, soccer, and swimming. They offer party packages that include access to the gym or pool, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a bounce house and a party room.

4. Coastal Carolina Skate Park

Coastal Carolina Skate Park is a great place to host a birthday party for skateboarders and BMX riders. They offer ramps, rails, and other obstacles for riders to use. They offer party packages that include access to the park, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a private lesson and a party room.

5. The Barnyard

The Barnyard is a petting zoo that offers a fun and unique experience for kids. They have a variety of animals, including goats, sheep, pigs, and donkeys. They offer party packages that include access to the petting zoo, pony rides, and a private party area. You can also add extras like face painting and a bounce house.

6. Onslow Pines Park

Onslow Pines Park is a great place to host a birthday party for nature lovers. They offer hiking trails, fishing ponds, and picnic areas. They offer party packages that include access to the park, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a nature scavenger hunt and a party room.

7. Adventure Landing

Adventure Landing is a family fun center that offers a variety of attractions, including mini golf, go-karts, laser tag, and an arcade. They offer party packages that include access to the attractions, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a private party room and a game card.

8. Skate Nation

Skate Nation is a roller skating rink that offers a fun and active birthday party experience. They offer party packages that include skate rental, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a private party room and a DJ.

9. Beach Bingo

Beach Bingo is a great place to host a birthday party for all ages. They offer bingo games, arcade games, and a snack bar. They offer party packages that include bingo games, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a private party room and a game card.

10. Coastal Carolina Curling Club

The Coastal Carolina Curling Club offers a unique and exciting birthday party experience for all ages. They offer curling lessons and games, pizza, and drinks. You can also add extras like a private party room and a Curling Olympics competition.

Conclusion

There are plenty of great places to have a birthday party in Jacksonville, NC. Whether you're looking for an indoor playground, a petting zoo, or a roller skating rink, there's something for everyone. With these top 10 places to choose from, you're sure to find the perfect spot to celebrate your special day.
