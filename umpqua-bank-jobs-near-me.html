Umpqua Bank is a community bank headquartered in Portland, Oregon. It has over 250 branches across the Western United States. Umpqua Bank offers a range of financial services to individuals, businesses, and non-profit organizations.

Umpqua Bank is known for its unique approach to banking. The bank has a strong commitment to its customers and communities. It also values its employees and offers a range of benefits and opportunities for career growth.

Umpqua Bank offers a range of job opportunities across different job functions. Some of the most common job roles at Umpqua Bank include customer service representatives, loan officers, and branch managers.

The requirements for Umpqua Bank jobs vary depending on the job role. However, some of the common requirements for most jobs include a high school diploma or equivalent, excellent communication skills, and a customer-focused approach.
